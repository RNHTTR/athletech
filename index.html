Athletech

Connecting student athletes with code

Mission

Athletech was founded to connect inherently hard working people with opportunities in technology that they don't have or are unaware of. Our goal is to create a talent pipeline that ultimately transforms student athletes from high school standouts into promising young technologists.

The idea is simple: Outstanding upperclassmen at the University of South Florida will facilitate the learning of foundational computer science topics (e.g. coding) to local high school football teams three times a week during a summer semester. The high school student athletes who demonstrate sufficient commitment and progress will be granted scholarships to USF or Hillsborough Community College, and the USF tutors will be connected with our hiring partners and will be guaranteed at least one job offer.
